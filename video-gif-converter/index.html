Video and gif converter

Tap for details

This app will convert videos and images to other formats (e.g., video to GIF) on your device. It does not upload your data anywhere. Since it uses your device's processor, it may take a long time for large videos (i.e., long videos or videos in high resolutions) depending on your device's computing power.

The embeds ffmpeg in your browser using ffmpeg.wasm. ffmpeg has too many options to show all of them in a simple UI, so I've tried to select only the most useful options. Feedback on other options to add is welcome, but since I'm a hobbyist doing this for free I can't guarantee I'll get around to every request.
